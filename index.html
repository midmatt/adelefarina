About

Architect of
Event Ecosystems

Nearly 30 years building event programs that move the business forward — from 10-person boardroom roundtables to 15,000-attendee international conferences.

Born and raised in the Bronx as one of seven sisters — bringing New Yorker grit and global perspective to every room she enters.

Adele Farina architects event ecosystems that perform at the intersection of brand, pipeline, and commercial strategy. Over 20 years leading complex, matrixed organizations, she has built Centers of Excellence from the ground up — establishing governance frameworks, technology infrastructure, and cross-functional operating models that transform fragmented event activity into unified, measurable growth engines.

At ION, she oversees 250–300 global events annually across six countries and eight business portfolios, including flagship client conferences, high-profile tradeshows like AWS re:Invent and FIA Boca, and exclusive executive programs. Her centralized technology platform spanning Cvent, Salesforce, Power BI, Power Apps, NetSuite, and Concur delivers real-time ROI attribution and executive dashboards used for annual budget allocation.

Beyond logistics and strategy, Adele is a purpose-driven leader who champions authenticity, mentorship, and people-first leadership — building trust across generations and inspiring teams to thrive.
